Before you buy

Do credits expire?+

No. Once you buy credits they stay on your account until you use them. Each individual posting runs 60 days from the date you publish it.

How long does each posting stay live?+

60 days from publish. We send a reminder email 7 days before expiry so you can renew it (using another credit) or let it close. Expired postings can be relisted at any time.

Can I get a refund?+

Yes — we offer a 15-day Satisfaction Guarantee on unused credits. If your posting doesn't go live as described due to a platform issue, contact support for a full refund within 15 days of purchase.

Are GST / HST / PST taxes included?+

Listed prices are base prices in CAD. Applicable GST/HST/PST is calculated at checkout based on your billing province and shown on your receipt.

Can I buy more credits later?+

Yes. You can buy additional credits from your dashboard at any time — they'll stack on top of your existing balance. Bulk packs give a better per-post rate, so hiring teams often buy a 10- or 30-pack upfront.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026 · Governed by PIPEDA and applicable provincial privacy legislation
🍁All personal data is stored on servers located in Canada, in compliance with PIPEDA.

1. Who We Are

YouthHire ("we", "us") is a Canadian job board platform operated under the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and applicable provincial privacy laws.

2. What We Collect

  • Name, email address, phone number (optional)
  • Company name, province, industry
  • Job posting content you create
  • Payment information — processed by our PCI-compliant payment processor. We do not store full card numbers.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, usage logs for security purposes

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the YouthHire platform and manage your account
  • To process payments and issue receipts
  • To send account-related emails (password reset, listing expiry notices)
  • To comply with Canadian tax law (GST/HST reporting obligations)
  • To detect fraud and enforce our Terms of Service
  • To send optional marketing emails — only if you explicitly opt in

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data only with: our payment processor (PCI DSS certified), email infrastructure providers under confidentiality agreements, and Canadian law enforcement or regulators when legally required.

5. Job Seeker Emails

When a job seeker clicks "Apply via Email," their email client opens with the employer's address pre-filled. We do not intercept, store, or process job seeker email communications.

6. Data Retention

We retain account data for the duration of your account plus 7 years for tax and legal compliance. Closed job postings are retained for 2 years, then deleted. You may request deletion of your account at any time.

7. Your Rights (PIPEDA)

  • Right to access your personal information
  • Right to correct inaccurate information
  • Right to withdraw consent
  • Right to request deletion (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Right to lodge a complaint with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada

8. Cookies

We use only essential session cookies. No tracking or advertising cookies are used.

9. Contact

Privacy inquiries: hello@canadayouthhire.ca. We respond within 30 days as required by PIPEDA.

About YouthHire

Canada's youth employment platform — connecting students, new grads, and young workers with employers hiring for entry-level, part-time, and first-job roles.

Built for Canada's Next Generation

YouthHire is built for young Canadians taking their first real step into the workforce. We focus on roles that matter for young workers — entry-level positions, part-time work that fits around school, co-ops, summer jobs, and early-career opportunities where a first yes can change everything.

We believe the first few jobs in a person's life do more than pay the bills — they shape habits, confidence, and a sense of what's possible. This platform exists to make that first step easier on both sides of the hire.

🤝

Our Commitment to Young Workers

Youth unemployment in Canada sits well above the national average. A big reason is simple — young workers need a first job to get a next job, and too many employers filter them out before they ever get a chance. YouthHire is built to shift that.

  • We prioritize postings open to entry-level, part-time, and no-experience-required candidates
  • We partner with schools, student unions, and community youth programs to make listings visible where young job seekers actually look
  • We push back on postings that require years of experience for jobs that shouldn't — and we update our practices when young workers tell us we can do better
  • We commit to transparency about our progress and our limitations
🌱

Students, New Grads & First-Job Seekers

Whether you're looking for your first part-time gig while studying, a summer job, a co-op placement, or the first role after graduation — YouthHire is built around the kinds of jobs young Canadians actually want to apply for.

Employers posting on YouthHire can reach motivated young workers ready to learn, show up, and grow with the role.

Frequently asked questions

Is YouthHire free for job seekers?+

Yes. Searching and applying for jobs on YouthHire is completely free. We never charge job seekers for access, applications, or recommendations.

Who is YouthHire for?+

YouthHire is built for young Canadians starting their work lives — high school and post-secondary students, new grads, and first-time job seekers. Employers posting here should expect motivated candidates early in their careers, not 10-year veterans.

What kinds of jobs are posted?+

Part-time work that fits around school, summer jobs, co-op and internship placements, entry-level full-time roles, and first-career positions. We focus on roles that genuinely welcome young workers — postings requiring years of prior experience aren't a fit here.

Do I need experience to apply?+

Most roles on YouthHire are open to applicants with little or no prior work experience. Each posting lists its requirements clearly, and we encourage employers to spell out what they're genuinely looking for — not aspirational wish-lists.

Where are the jobs located?+

Across Canada — every province and territory. You can filter by city, province, or remote work. Many postings are hybrid or fully remote, making them easier to combine with school.

How much does it cost to post a job?+

Employers buy credits and spend one credit per posting. New employers get 5 free posting credits at signup during our grand opening. See the Pricing page for current rates and volume discounts.

How long does a posting stay live?+

Each posting runs until its expiry date (set by the employer at posting time). We send the employer a reminder 7 days before expiry so they can renew or let it close.
